About Sarasota Community Hub

Our Mission

The Sarasota Community Resource Hub is dedicated to connecting residents with essential services, programs, and opportunities available in our community. We believe that access to information about community resources is crucial for building a strong, connected, and thriving Sarasota.

Our goal is to make it easy for residents to find the help they need, when they need it. Whether you're looking for healthcare, job training, food assistance, or community programs, our hub provides a comprehensive directory of local resources.
